About LambdaList

LambdaList was created by various cohorts at Lambda School, their goal was to create an app that helps you organize your tasks. LambdaList will help you organize by allowing you to create tasks to oragnize your life. Set reminders or automaically set repeat tasks, LambdaList will help you check everything off your list!
